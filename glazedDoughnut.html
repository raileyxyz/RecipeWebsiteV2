Glazed Doughnut

Soft and airy doughnuts coated in a shiny sugar glaze. Sweet, light, and melts in your mouth with every bite. A perfect treat to pair with coffee or milk.

Procedures

  1. Warm the milk to about 110°F (43°C).
  2. Combine warm milk, yeast, and sugar in a bowl. Stir gently and let sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy.
  3. In another bowl, whisk eggs, melted butter, vanilla extract, and nutmeg.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the yeast mixture and mix well.
  5. Add part of the flour and salt, mix until combined.
  6. Gradually add the remaining flour until a soft dough forms.
  7. Knead the dough for 5–7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  8. Form into a ball, place in a greased bowl, and cover.
  9. Let rise 1½–2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  10. Punch down the dough, roll out to ½ inch thick.
  11. Cut into doughnut shapes using cutters.
  12. Place on a tray, cover, and let rise again for 30–45 minutes.
  13. Heat oil to 350°F (177°C) and fry doughnuts 1 minute per side until golden.
  14. Drain on paper towels or wire rack.
  15. For the glaze: mix confectioners’ sugar, milk or cream, and vanilla extract.
  16. Dip warm doughnuts into glaze and let excess drip off.
  17. Place on rack to allow glaze to set before serving.
