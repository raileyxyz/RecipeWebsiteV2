Choco Muffins

Soft, fluffy chocolate muffins that are rich and flavorful. Loaded with chocolate chunks for an extra burst of sweetness. Ideal for breakfast, snacks, or dessert

All Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Plain Flour (all-purpose flour) 0
Baking Soda 0
Salt 0
Dutch Process Cocoa Powder 0
Instant Coffee (optional) 0
Hot Full-Fat Milk 0
Canola Oil 0
Brown Sugar 0
Vanilla Extract 0
Sour Cream / Yogurt 0
Large Egg 0
Chocolate Chips 0

Procedures

  1. Prepare your tools and ingredients.
  2. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F) and line muffin tray with paper liners.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients: flour, baking soda, salt, cocoa powder, coffee (optional).
  4. Dissolve instant coffee in hot milk (if used) and let cool slightly.
  5. Combine wet ingredients: oil, brown sugar, vanilla extract, sour cream/yogurt, egg. Mix until smooth.
  6. Add milk mixture to wet ingredients. Stir gently.
  7. Gradually add dry mixture to wet mixture. Stir until just combined. Do not overmix.
  8. Fold in chocolate chips evenly.
  9. Fill muffin cups ¾ full and bake for 15–20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  10. Cool muffins in tray 5 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely before serving.
