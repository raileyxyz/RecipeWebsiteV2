Brownies

Moist and fudgy chocolate brownies baked to perfection. They have a deep cocoa flavor and a slightly crisp top layer. A delightful dessert that satisfies every chocolate craving

All Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Butter (melted) 0
Dark Chocolate Chips (melted) 0
All Purpose Cream 0
White Sugar 0
Brown Sugar 0
Egg 0
Vanilla Extract 0
All Purpose Flour 0
Fine Salt 0
Cocoa Powder 0
Semi-sweet Chocolate Chips (optional) 0

Procedures

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 320Â°F.
  2. Using a whisk, mix melted butter, melted chocolate, and All Purpose Cream. Stir in white and brown sugar. Mix until smooth (2 minutes).
  3. Whisk in the eggs one at a time. Add vanilla extract and whisk until smooth (3 minutes).
  4. Sift flour, salt, and cocoa powder in a separate bowl. Add the dry ingredient mix to the wet ingredients until just combined. Do not overmix (3 minutes).
  5. Grease a baking pan and line with aluminum foil. Pour the mixture then top with semi-sweet chocolate. Bake for 20 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean when inserted in the middle.
  6. Set aside to cool completely. Remove from the pan and peel off the aluminum foil. Cut into serving pieces and serve (10 minutes).
