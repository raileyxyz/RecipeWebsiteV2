Choco Cupcake

Deliciously moist chocolate cupcakes with smooth, creamy frosting. Each bite is a mix of sweetness and rich cocoa flavor. Perfect for parties or a sweet treat at home.

Procedures

  1. Prepare materials and ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and line the cupcake pan with paper liners.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Combine wet ingredients: buttermilk, oil, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Stir until blended.
  5. Pour wet mixture into the dry ingredients. Mix gently until smooth and lump-free (avoid overmixing).
  6. Fill cupcake liners about ¾ full with batter.
  7. Bake for 12–18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Cool cupcakes in the tray for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack. Decorate if desired.
